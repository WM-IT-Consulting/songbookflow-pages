Privacy Policy

SongbookFlow · Last updated July 16, 2026

SongbookFlow ("the app") is developed by Walace Morais. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it's used.

Data we collect

  • Account identity: email address and, if provided, your name — from Sign in with Apple, Google, or Spotify OAuth (via Supabase Auth), used to create and secure your SongbookFlow account.
  • Third-party service credentials: if you connect CifraClub or MultiTracks.com, we send the email and password you enter directly to that service's login system to authenticate you. See "Third-party credentials" below for retention details.
  • Music library access: with your permission, the app reads your Apple Music library to let you select playlists to convert.
  • Usage analytics: anonymized product usage events (e.g. screens viewed, conversion actions) via PostHog, to help us understand how the app is used and fix problems.
  • Advertising data: the app shows ads via Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data under Google's own privacy policy.

Third-party credentials

CifraClub and MultiTracks.com don't support signing in through Apple, Google, or Spotify, so SongbookFlow asks for your email and password for those services directly. This is used only to authenticate your session with that service on your behalf, to sync your songbooks and setlists. We do not sell or share these credentials with anyone else.

How we use data

  • To operate core app functionality: matching and converting playlists, and syncing them to your connected destinations.
  • To maintain your account and session across devices (via Supabase).
  • To understand product usage and improve the app (via PostHog analytics).
  • To show ads that help keep the app free (via Google AdMob).

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with the service providers necessary to operate the app: Supabase (backend/auth), PostHog (analytics), Google AdMob (advertising), and the streaming/songbook services you explicitly connect (Spotify, Apple Music, CifraClub, MultiTracks.com).

Your choices

You can disconnect any connected account at any time in Settings. To request deletion of your SongbookFlow account and associated data, email walacepmorais@gmail.com.
