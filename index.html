Frequently Asked Questions

What does SongbookFlow do?
It connects a source (Spotify, Apple Music, or YouTube Music) to CifraClub, then matches and converts your playlist tracks into a CifraClub songbook.

A track didn't match correctly. What do I do?
Open the converted playlist in your Library and use the re-match option on the unmatched or low-confidence track to search and pick the correct one.

Why does SongbookFlow ask for my CifraClub login?
CifraClub doesn't support Sign in with Apple/Google, so the app signs in with your email and password directly to sync songbooks to your own account. Credentials are sent only to CifraClub to authenticate you and are not stored by SongbookFlow beyond your active session unless noted otherwise in the Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my account or data?
Email walacepmorais@gmail.com with your account email and we'll remove your data within 30 days.
